Child Safety & CSAM Policy

App: Rendezvous (app.rendezvous.social)

Last updated: August 25, 2025

Zero Tolerance

Rendezvous maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse material (CSAM), child exploitation, grooming, or any conduct that endangers minors. Such content and behavior are prohibited on our platform. We promptly remove and report CSAM and any suspected child exploitation to the appropriate authorities and hotlines, consistent with applicable laws and platform policies (including Google Play’s requirements).

Report a Concern

If you encounter CSAM or behavior that may endanger a child in the app, report it immediately:

  • Email: efwfew1611@gmail.com
  • In-app: Use any available report/flag feature on profiles, messages, or posts.

Include links, screenshots, and any details that can help us locate the content quickly. We acknowledge receipt, review urgently, and take appropriate action.

Enforcement Actions

Upon detection or credible report, we may: remove content; suspend or terminate accounts and devices; preserve and provide necessary data to law enforcement upon valid legal requests; and notify relevant hotlines or authorities.

Safety by Design

  • Proactive moderation workflows to detect and triage violations.
  • User controls to report and block abusive users.
  • Policy and reviewer training focused on child safety.

Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable laws and cooperate with lawful investigations. When required, we report CSAM to the appropriate authority or hotline in the relevant jurisdiction. We preserve evidence consistent with legal obligations.
